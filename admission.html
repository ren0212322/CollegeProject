Admission Process

Admissions to various programs are based on entrance exams like SNUET, WBJEE, GMAT, and NATA, depending on the course.

The application process can be completed online through the university's official website.

Eligibility Criteria

Undergraduate Programs: Candidates must have passed their 12th standard with required subjects.

Postgraduate Programs: A relevant bachelor's degree is required.

Scholarships

The university offers merit-based scholarships that can cover up to 100% of tuition fees based on academic performance.
